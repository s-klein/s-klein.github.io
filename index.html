Experience

Sam Klein is an independent researcher and writer contributing to magazines on topics at the intersection of cybersecurity, open-source technology, and digital infrastructure. With over a decade of professional experience -- including six years as a cybersecurity engineer and four years as a systems engineer, administrator, and technician -- he has worked across enterprise, academic, and start-up environments, focusing on resilient systems, Linux platforms, and applied security practices. His recent projects and publications explore baseline configurations, vulnerability analysis, and innovative methods for detecting malicious software, while his current work bridges technical expertise with accessible commentary for broader audiences.
